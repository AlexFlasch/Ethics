Ethics Scenario

by: Alex Flasch

Renegade Path

“Sure! That shouldn’t be any problem at all! I can get that done pretty quickly.” You respond.

“Great! I was hoping that would be your answer! How about another drink on me!?”

You agree to implement the backdoor. You ponder the fact that it nearly defeats the purpose of the encryption in the first place, and on top of that, it seems to go against the company’s ethics of listening to their community. The backdoor surely wouldn’t go over well with its users and may even completely eliminate the userbase if word that Connekt has a backdoor gets out and the reaction is strong enough. However, you love your job, and you don’t want to risk meeting a similar fate to Jackson.

Luckily though, the info about the backdoor being implemented is very unlikely to get out to the public and you’ll probably forget about the whole situation within a few months. Heck, maybe the community would find out and, while the reaction would be bad, it would blow over within a few weeks anyways.
