Ethics Scenario

by: Alex Flasch

My Choice

Personally, I would choose option B2. I would certainly not feel comfortable putting a backdoor into an encryption system, especially one that I’ve worked tirelessly on. I would try and work something out with my boss to see if there was an option to avoid implementing it, but if there were no other option I don’t think I would be able to continue working at such a place. I think it’s important for companies to stand by their ethics, whatever they may be. Especially when they have a large, dedicated userbase that could easily move to an alternative piece of software.
